Techniques

Composting

4.5 (128 reviews)

Transform organic waste into nutrient-rich soil amendment. Layer green and brown materials in 1:3 ratio, maintain moisture, and turn regularly.

Benefits: Improves soil, reduces waste, natural fertilizer

Time: 3-12 months

Materials: Kitchen scraps, yard waste, water

Natural Pest Control

4.0 (95 reviews)

Use beneficial insects, companion planting, and organic sprays to manage pests without chemicals.

Benefits: No chemicals, preserves ecosystem

Methods: Neem oil, ladybugs, companion plants

Best for: Vegetables, fruits, flowers
